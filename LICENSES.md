# Licences And Rights Boundary

Antica Fiamma is a mixed-rights digital research object. The MIT licence recorded as the primary Zenodo licence applies to project-owned software code, not automatically to every item visible on the live site or mentioned in this archive.

## Project-Owned Software

Unless a file states otherwise, source code written for Antica Fiamma in this release is available under the MIT License. See [`LICENSE`](LICENSE).

## Project Documentation

Documentation written by Ruoci Song in this release is available under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International licence (CC BY 4.0), unless a file states otherwise.

## Rights-Safe Samples And Derived Metadata

Files under [`sample_runtime/`](sample_runtime/) use synthetic, redacted, or aggregate content to document data structures and runtime scale. They may be reused as project documentation under CC BY 4.0. This permission does not grant rights in any source material or in the complete runtime corpus from which aggregate counts were calculated.

Project-generated schemas and metadata may be reused only where their files contain no protected source text and no third-party content subject to a narrower notice. File-level notices control if present.

## Screenshots, Images, And Interface Captures

Project-created interface screenshots and route-tour images may be cited or reproduced for scholarly discussion with attribution to Antica Fiamma and Ruoci Song. A screenshot may display brief source-derived excerpts, labels, or metadata. Reuse must preserve source attribution and must not be used to reconstruct or redistribute a protected commentary corpus. Third-party images, fonts, or other assets remain subject to their own terms.

## Dartmouth Dante Project-Derived Commentary

Antica Fiamma uses the Dartmouth Dante Project (DDP) as the visible commentary source archive. The project author holds written authorisation for the current academic, non-profit website use of DDP-derived material.

That authorisation is limited to the current website use. It is not represented here as a licence to redistribute the complete DDP-derived commentary payload through GitHub, Zenodo, or another corpus repository. The complete payload is therefore excluded from this release. Rights in commentary texts remain with their applicable rights holders and source contexts.

The GitHub repository contains generated interface pages used by the authorised live website. Their inclusion as deployable website outputs does not place source-derived content under the repository's MIT or CC BY licences. The narrower Zenodo 1.0.0 archive excludes generated author/work/personaggio pages that may cumulatively expose source-derived material.

## Dante Text And Other Source Materials

Dante's text and any public-domain source examples retain their source and edition attributions. Public-domain status of one layer does not alter the rights status of adjacent commentary, images, editions, translations, or metadata.

## No Expansion By Aggregation

Nothing in the repository licence, Zenodo licence field, archive packaging, or public availability of the live site expands a narrower third-party permission. When licence scopes conflict, the narrower source-specific restriction controls for the affected material.
