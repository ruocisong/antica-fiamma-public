San Pietro belongs here because in Paradiso he is not just apostolic authority residue: he is a figure with voice, judgment, and scene-presence.

Why This Split Matters

The autore room tracks commentary authority usage. The personaggio page tracks Peter as a speaking apostolic presence inside the poem.

This page marks the fact that Peter belongs to both theological authority and Paradise drama.

A related autore page already exists at autore/san_pietro.html.

Scene Note

Apostolic scene-presence tied to testing, judgment, and Paradiso intensity.

Main dramatic corridor: Paradiso 24 to 27.

San Pietro's room matters because apostolic authority and dramatic judgment strike at the same time: he is not only cited as first apostle but staged as the figure who tests, burns, and measures truth in the celestial corridor.
