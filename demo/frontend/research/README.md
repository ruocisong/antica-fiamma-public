# Public Research Rooms

This directory contains public research rooms for **Antica Fiamma**.

Research rooms are not generic blog posts. They are focused public case studies built inside the larger reading environment. Each room should connect a research question back to Dante's poem, the commentary tradition, and the relevant interface paths.

## Current Rooms

- [`fiamma.html`](./fiamma.html): Dante and the fire motif in the *Commedia*.

The Fiamma room is supported by static assets under [`../static/research`](../static/research).

## What Belongs Here

Use this directory for polished, public-facing research rooms that are safe to publish as part of the site shell.

A research room can include:

- interpretive framing
- small public datasets or tables
- static visualizations
- links back into the line-first interface
- notes on method and limits

## What Does Not Belong Here

Do not use this directory for:

- private drafts
- raw notebooks
- large generated data dumps
- internal review packets
- unpublished publication materials
- scratch visual experiments not linked from the public site

Those materials belong in the internal project workspace, not in the public repository.
