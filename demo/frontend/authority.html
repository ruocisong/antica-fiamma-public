What This Layer Is

The Authority Layer does not simply collect cited names. It reorganises commentary use into a line-first route: Line for local accountability, Canto Map for canto-wide distribution, and Full Authority Page for author or personaggio rooms. Its job is to make authority usage readable as a navigable tradition rather than as a residue hidden inside record cards.

In practical terms, that means the layer now supports multiple levels of objecthood: record-local work mentions on the selected line, per-canto authority indexes, fully shaped objects such as Aristotle or Virgil, a tracked author universe, and personaggi that can be entered through poem hits, commentary aliases, and local figure bands.

The key design decision is that authority remains anchored to the reading scene. You do not have to leave line, card, or commentary language behind in order to enter authors, works, personaggi, or preserved commentary records.

How To Enter It

There are now three honest scopes. Line begins from the selected verse and the commentary records that touch it. Canto Map widens to the full canto and shows where authority signals gather. Full Authority Page opens the larger author or personaggio room when you need the whole object.

That plurality is important because authority use does not arrive in only one scholarly form. Sometimes you start from a cited name, sometimes from a figure in the poem, sometimes from a work chip such as Vita Nuova or Convivio, and sometimes from one preserved commentary record opened in the reader.

Line Scope

Line scope keeps the selected verse still. Work chips open filtered record lists inside the Authority module, and Open in reader opens a single commentary record without moving the main entry away from the line.

Author Entry

Readiness no longer depends on pretending every author has the same structure. Some enter through the same works-tree structure but still preserve different entry contracts, especially where author/personaggio pressure or commentary-sensitive scope must remain explicit.

Commentary Highlights

Authority highlights now keep authority-reading close to the page. This matters because the Authority universe should not live only in a side panel; it should also become visible where the commentary language itself starts invoking it.

Object Types

The layer no longer treats shallowness as the main distinction. The major live distinction is between local line evidence, canto-wide distribution, and larger rooms whose reading contract still needs to say how much text, commentary, work, or personaggio evidence is mounted.

Mature Structures

Authors such as Aristotle, Paul, Augustine, Virgil, Ovid, or Cicero already support deeper drilldown, pressure/frontline metadata, and more explicit entry contracts.

Scoped Rooms

Some rooms still need scoped reading discipline even after work data is available. This is most visible where commentary pressure, role pressure, or double identity should remain explicit rather than being flattened into an ordinary browse tree.

Still Uneven

The point is not to suppress internal unevenness but to state clearly where the remaining difference now lives. In Antica Fiamma it lives less in a simple works-tree yes/no, and more in entry contract, scoped caveat, and reading pressure.

Boundaries

This page does not claim that every cited name is already solved. It claims something more useful: the layer now has enough scale, discipline, and entry logic to deserve a page of its own.

The next frontier is therefore not blind expansion. It is to keep thickening reading contracts, clickable line and canto routes, work-level drilldown, and commentary-integrated authority paths for the universe that already exists.

In other words, the design priority is not to hide unevenness but to keep it legible. Mature objects, thinner objects, and special-case objects should all remain readable as what they really are.

这层到底是什么

Authority Layer 不是简单地把被引用的人名堆在一起。它把 commentary 里的 authority 使用重新组织成一条 line-first 路径：Line 负责局部核查，Canto Map 展开整支 canto 的分布，Full Authority Page 进入 author 或 personaggio 房间。这样 authority tradition 可以被走、被读、被分层，而不只是 cards 里的残余。

所以这里现在已经有多种对象形态：选中 line 上的 record-local work mentions、逐 canto 的 authority indexes、像 Aristotele、Virgilio 这种更成熟的对象、完整的 tracked author universe，以及可以通过 poem hits、commentary aliases 和本地 bands 被打开的 personaggi。

这层最关键的设计决定，是 authority 始终要锚在阅读现场上。你不需要为了进入 authors、works、personaggi 或 preserved commentary records，就把 line、card 和 commentary 语言整个丢在身后。

怎么进入

现在有三个诚实尺度：Line 从选中的诗行和覆盖它的 commentary records 开始；Canto Map 展开整支 canto 的 authority 分布；Full Authority Page 在你需要完整对象时进入更大的 author 或 personaggio room。

这种复数入口很重要，因为 authority 使用本来就不会只以一种学术形态到来。有时你是从被引用的人名开始，有时是从诗里的人物开始，有时是从 Vita Nuova 或 Convivio 这样的 work chip 开始，有时则是从 reader 里打开的一条 preserved commentary record 开始。

Line scope

Line scope 会让 main entry 停在当前诗行。work chips 在 Authority 模块内部打开 filtered record list；Open in reader 打开单条 commentary 全文，不把整页从这一行挪走。

Author Entry

现在的 ready 不再意味着每个 author 都必须长成同一种结构。更真实的差别，已经不是“有没有 works tree”，而是某些 author 是否仍需保留 role pressure、commentary-sensitive scope 或 double identity 这类更谨慎的入口契约。

Commentary Highlights

Authority 高亮现在让 authority 阅读贴回正文。这很重要，因为 Authority universe 不该只活在侧栏，也应该开始成为评论语言本身的一层可见结构。

对象类型

当前这层已经不需要再把“有没有 works tree”当成主要差别。现在更真实的差别，是 local line evidence、canto-wide distribution，以及更大的 author/personaggio room 各自能支撑到什么强度。

成熟结构

像 Aristotele、Paolo Apostolo、Agostino、Virgilio、Ovidio、Cicerone 这些对象，现在已经支持更深的 drilldown、pressure/frontline metadata 和更清楚的 entry contract。

Scoped Rooms

有些 room 即使已经挂出了 work data，仍然需要保留 scoped reading discipline。最明显的是那些带有 commentary pressure、作者/人物双重身份，或更敏感范围契约的对象。

仍然不均匀

这里要保留的不是“延后”本身，而是差异真实落在哪里。在 Antica Fiamma 里，它越来越少地体现在 works-tree yes/no，而更多地体现在 entry contract、scoped caveat 和阅读压力上。这种诚实是这层设计的一部分。

边界

这页不声称每一个被提到的名字都已经被解决。它声称的是另一件更有用的事：Authority 这一层现在已经长到足以拥有自己的页面、自己的入口逻辑、自己的阅读契约。

所以下一步最值的，不再是盲扩，而是继续把已经存在的 universe 做厚：clickable line / canto 路径、更深的 work drilldown、commentary-integrated authority path、以及更稳定的前端 entry copy。

换句话说，这层现在最重要的，不是把不均匀藏起来，而是继续把它说清楚。成熟对象、较薄对象、以及 special-case 对象，都应该作为它们真实的样子被读到。
