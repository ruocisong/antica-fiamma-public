What This Layer Is

The Authority Layer does not simply collect cited names. It reorganises commentary use into local authority profiles, work-level entry lanes, personaggio rooms, and commentary-source bridges. Its job is to make authority usage readable as a navigable tradition rather than as a residue hidden inside record cards.

In practical terms, that means the layer now supports multiple levels of objecthood: fully shaped objects such as Aristotle or Virgil, a full tracked author universe, and personaggi that can now be entered through poem hits, commentary aliases, and local figure bands.

The key design decision is that authority remains anchored to the reading scene. You do not have to leave line, card, or commentary language behind in order to enter authors, works, or source passages.

How To Enter It

There are now three honest entry routes. One begins from the Authority panel itself. One begins from the Compare workspace, where shared authority usage appears between pinned cards. One begins inside the commentary text, where highlighted author and work surfaces now start to signal the same authority universe directly in the reading flow.

That plurality is important because authority use does not arrive in only one scholarly form. Sometimes you start from a cited name, sometimes from a figure in the poem, and sometimes from a recurrent work or source habit emerging inside commentary prose.

Authority Lens

The Lens should be read as a staged entry, not as a flat filter. The contract banner, drilldown lane, and bucket notes now explain what kind of object you are entering and which layer of the completed authority universe you are opening.

Author Entry

Readiness no longer depends on pretending every author has the same structure. Some enter through the same works-tree structure but still preserve different entry contracts, especially where author/personaggio pressure or commentary-sensitive scope must remain explicit.

Commentary Highlights

Authority highlights now keep authority-reading close to the page. This matters because the Authority universe should not live only in a side panel; it should also become visible where the commentary language itself starts invoking it.

Object Types

The layer no longer needs to pretend that shallower structure is the main distinction. The major live distinction is now between a broad works-tree universe and a smaller set of rooms whose reading contract still needs extra caveat.

Mature Structures

Authors such as Aristotle, Paul, Augustine, Virgil, Ovid, or Cicero already support deeper drilldown, pressure/frontline metadata, and more explicit entry contracts.

Scoped Rooms

Some rooms still need scoped reading discipline even after the works tree is available. This is most visible where commentary pressure, role pressure, or double identity should remain explicit rather than being flattened into an ordinary browse tree.

Still Uneven

The point is not to suppress internal unevenness but to state clearly where the remaining difference now lives. In Segni de l’antica fiamma it lives less in the presence or absence of works trees, and more in entry contract, scoped caveat, and reading pressure.

Boundaries

This page does not claim that every cited name is already solved. It claims something more useful: the layer now has enough scale, discipline, and entry logic to deserve a page of its own.

The next frontier is therefore not blind expansion. It is to keep thickening reading contracts, clickable entry routes, work-level drilldown, and commentary-integrated authority paths for the universe that already exists.

In other words, the design priority is not to hide unevenness but to keep it legible. Mature objects, thinner objects, and special-case objects should all remain readable as what they really are.

这层到底是什么

Authority Layer 不是简单地把被引用的人名堆在一起。它把 commentary 里的 authority 使用重新组织成 author profile、work-level entry、personaggio rooms 和 commentary-source bridge，让 authority tradition 可以被走、被读、被分层，而不只是 cards 里的残余。

所以这里现在已经有多种对象形态：像 Aristotele、Virgilio 这种更成熟的对象；完整的 tracked author universe；以及可以通过 poem hits、commentary aliases 和本地 bands 被打开的 personaggi。

这层最关键的设计决定，是 authority 始终要锚在阅读现场上。你不需要为了进入 authors、works 或 source passages，就把 line、card 和 commentary 语言整个丢在身后。

怎么进入

现在有三条诚实入口：一条是从 Authority panel 自己进去，一条是从 Compare 里共享 authority 使用进去，一条是从 commentary 正文里的高亮 surface 进去。

这种复数入口很重要，因为 authority 使用本来就不会只以一种学术形态到来。有时你是从被引用的人名开始，有时是从诗里的人物开始，有时则是从 commentary prose 里反复浮现的 work 或 source 习惯开始。

Authority Lens

Lens 现在不该被读成一个平铺 filter，而该被读成 staged entry。contract banner、drilldown lane 和 bucket note 都在说明：你现在进入的是哪种对象，以及你打开的是 authority universe 的哪一层。

Author Entry

现在的 ready 不再意味着每个 author 都必须长成同一种结构。更真实的差别，已经不是“有没有 works tree”，而是某些 author 是否仍需保留 role pressure、commentary-sensitive scope 或 double identity 这类更谨慎的入口契约。

Commentary Highlights

Authority 高亮现在让 authority 阅读贴回正文。这很重要，因为 Authority universe 不该只活在侧栏，也应该开始成为评论语言本身的一层可见结构。

对象类型

当前这层已经不需要再把“有没有 works tree”当成主要差别。现在更真实的差别，是哪些 room 可以直接按 works tree 读，哪些 room 仍需额外 caveat、scoped entry 和 staged explanation。

成熟结构

像 Aristotele、Paolo Apostolo、Agostino、Virgilio、Ovidio、Cicerone 这些对象，现在已经支持更深的 drilldown、pressure/frontline metadata 和更清楚的 entry contract。

Scoped Rooms

有些 room 即使已经挂出了 works tree，仍然需要保留 scoped reading discipline。最明显的是那些带有 commentary pressure、作者/人物双重身份，或更敏感范围契约的对象。

仍然不均匀

这里要保留的不是“延后”本身，而是差异真实落在哪里。在 Segni de l’antica fiamma 里，它越来越少地体现在“有没有 works tree”，而更多地体现在 entry contract、scoped caveat 和阅读压力上。这种诚实是这层设计的一部分。

边界

这页不声称每一个被提到的名字都已经被解决。它声称的是另一件更有用的事：Authority 这一层现在已经长到足以拥有自己的页面、自己的入口逻辑、自己的阅读契约。

所以下一步最值的，不再是盲扩，而是继续把已经存在的 universe 做厚：clickable 路径、更深的 work drilldown、commentary-integrated authority path、以及更稳定的前端 entry copy。

换句话说，这层现在最重要的，不是把不均匀藏起来，而是继续把它说清楚。成熟对象、较薄对象、以及 special-case 对象，都应该作为它们真实的样子被读到。
